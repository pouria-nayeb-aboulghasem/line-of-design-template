2033: An Optimistic Future

Type

Exhibition

Location

Toronto

Year

2023

A place to experience everyday life in an optimistic future.

Set in 2033 in the Pelham Cultural Hub, Mason Studio, Goodee and Azure, along with various collaborators, co-create a place where the care for people and our planet comes before all else, and we collectively empower communities to look at the future with excitement, curiosity, and joy.

The exhibition presents a hopeful vision for everyday living. A place to sustain our minds and body through learning, exploring creative pursuits, socializing, and trade.

In part of the DesignTO 2023 festival, the exhibition included a marketplace, a learning zone, a pay-what-you-want cafe, a kids' playspace, a fabrication workshop, a study lounge and a communal library.

The partners on the project include Aaline, Azure Magazine, Babatree, DesignTO, ecoBirdy, Fors Studio, Goodee, Humber College, In:Situ Plants, IZ Adaptive, Propeller Coffee, Toronto Metropolitan University.
